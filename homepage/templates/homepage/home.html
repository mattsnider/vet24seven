What the Vet24seven app does for you and your pet:

Provides you with personalized pet healthcare from licensed, screened veterinary professionals, using your mobile device.

Reduces pet and owner anxiety, especially cats and aging or chronically ill pets, by avoiding stressful trips to the vet clinic.

Brings a vet to your mobile device when you need one, at your convenience, for medical and behavioral issues, or general questions/advice in between your standard vet visits.

Helps keep your pet healthier and safer and provides you, as a pet owner, with peace of mind in the care your pet receives.

Allows the vet to see and experience what you do via photos, video, chat and the written description you provide via the app. The vet responds with a course of home-treatment for your pet.
